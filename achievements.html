6th National Conference on "Innovative Global Technology Trends in Art, Design, Technology, Management, Law, Vedic Science, Education, Architecture, Film & Media (IGT-ADTMLVSEAF&M)

The fusion of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and presentation design has birthed SlideCraft, a groundbreaking tool revolutionizing presentation creation. SlideCraft facilitates real-time collaboration through technology, ensuring low-latency communication and updates.
